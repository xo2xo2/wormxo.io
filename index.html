About the Game

Wormzilla.io was built for fun. Grow your worm by eating various foods on the map and power up with items that grant special abilities.

Support & VIP

To support the project and unlock new looks (plus upload your own custom skin) consider a VIP subscription for an ad-free experience. Ads help us cover costs and keep development going.

Classic, Reimagined

Wormzilla is entertainment-first — a modern take on the classic snake game you may remember from old mobile phones.

Community Guidelines

  • No discrimination of any kind (race, religion, language, color).
  • No harassment, hate speech, profanity, NSFW or terror content.
  • Respect other players and play fair.

Violations lead to permanent bans, even for VIP members.
